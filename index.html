What we do

Oligomaster offers a broad range of testing and characterization services related to polymers and coatings for product development, failure analysis, quality assurance and quality control. Oligomaster is using state-of-the-art, high-level polymer testing, analytical and mechanical procedures. A combination of analysis and physical testing locates problems and provides recommendations for solutions.

Test Your Material

We use a wide range of testing methodologies and instrumentation to perform our tests. These include light microscopy, 3D-microscopy, scanning electron microscopy (SEM), gas chromatography (GC), high pressure liquid chromatography (HPLC), gas chromatograph-mass spectrometer (GC-MS), fourier transform infrared (FTIR) spectrometers. As well as, thermal analysis including thermogravimetric analysis (TGA), dynamic scanning calorimetry (DSC) and dynamic mechanical analysis (DMA), heat aging, accelerated weathering testing, and more!

Expert Advice from Our R&D Team

Oligomaster has an experienced team of researchers and contracts research, product development and testing. Oligomaster is providing technical services to manufacturers of polymer-based products and coatings. Our breadth of capabilities allows us to offer comprehensive product and process development, testing and consulting services (including literature reviews) in a broad range of areas related to polymer technology. Our team can be involved in your product development from the beginning concept development, through to prototyping, optimisation and small scale production stages.
